Karbonn Mobiles is into the manufacturing of smartphones, tablets and mobile phone accessories. Karbonn Mobiles also present in countries like Nepal, Sri Lanka and in the Middle East and one of the leading Indian mobile company.

Lava International

Lava International is the multi-national Indian mobile company, dealing in the mobile handset industry in India as well as Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and the Middle East. Lava also own Xolo brand with Intel processor, also launched Windows Phone operating system and Android Lollipop smartphones.

Intex Technologies

Intex Technologies is the India’s second-largest selling mobile phone company and also into the LED TVs business.

I-Ball Mobile

Best IT World India consumer electronics company is known as iBall, mostly imports computer peripherals and also into the business of Laptop and Smartphones with collaboration with Intel and Microsoft.
